Aaron Swartz

A Very Brief History

Aaron Swartz was an American programmer and activist. He pushed for open access to information, in his Guerilla Open Access Manifesto he urged those with access to information to copy and share said information. He also urged everyone to take information and save, copy, and redistribute, and fight for Guerilla Open Access.

Aaron attended Stanford University but dropped out after one year. Despite this he still had an impact on the internet, he created web.py because he was unhappy with current web applications frameworks. Aaron also helped the creation of reddit, the 6th most visited site in the US, when his company Infogami merged with Reddit in its early days.
